Audio Files Required

The meditation app requires audio files to be placed in this directory.

Required Files:

  • nature-meditation.mp3 - Nature sounds for meditation
  • ambient-meditation.mp3 - Ambient music for relaxation
  • bells-meditation.mp3 - Meditation bells and chimes

Please add these files to the /public/audio/ directory for the meditation sound feature to work properly. Ideally, use audio files that:

  • Are 2-5 minutes long (they will loop)
  • Start and end smoothly for seamless looping
  • Are compressed to a reasonable quality (128-192kbps)

Where to find free meditation audio:

  • Freesound.org - Meditation
  • Pixabay Music - Meditation
  • Mixkit - Meditation

Remember to check licensing for any audio you use.
